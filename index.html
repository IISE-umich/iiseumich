About IISE at the University of Michigan


The Institute of Industrial and Systems Engineers (IISE) at the University of Michigan is a student-run organization dedicated to advancing the field of industrial engineering by supporting students with career-building opportunities.

  • Trainings and Workshops
  •  Professional Development Opportunities
  • Social Events for Networking
