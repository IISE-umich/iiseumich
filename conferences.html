IIse Annual Conference

About the Conference

The annual IISE conference brings together students and professionals from across the country to explore the latest in industrial and systems engineering.

Attendees gain valuable insights from keynote speakers, technical learning sessions, and network opportunities for students to grow academically and professionally.

At past IISE conferences, chapter members have participated in presenting research during the IISE research gallery. The 2025 IISE Annual Conference was held in Atlanta.


If you are interested in participating in the annual IISE Conference, please reach out to the IISE Board for more information: IISE-Board@umich.edu
