Join Us!

IISE at the University of Michigan is welcome to all students! While everyone is invited to participate in some of our general events, full access to core opportunities including trainings, special workshops, and select catered events require a membership. Please note that there is a membership fee of $44/year.


The IISE Michigan chapter provides scholarships and discounts. Please contact IISE-Board@umich.edu for any financial inquiries. We are here to help!

Step 1

Application

Fill out the online application linked below. This application is also linked in our instagram bio. Members can join at any time throughout the year.


Application 
Step 2

Acceptance

You will receive an email on behalf of the IISE board of your acceptance into the club. Congrats! Please proceed to step 3 to become an official member.

Step 3

Membership

In order to participate in special events, please create an IISE account and purchase a student membership using the link below. Send a screenshot of your receipt to 

IISE-Board@umich.edu
Membership 
Step 4

Setup and Communication

Come to our first informational session of the semester, or contact IISE-Board@umich.edu to set you up with our club communication platforms.

Contact IISE-Board@umich.edu if you have any questions or concerns.
