Events

Workshops and Seminars

Gain hands-on experience and industry insights through workshops led by professionals and alumni. Topics include resume building, certifications, elevator pitches, lean manufacturing, other IOE-related topics, and more.

Lean Green Six Sigma Training

Get introduced to process improvement methodologies used in top companies. This training equips members with practical tools in Lean and Six Sigma. This training is perfect for anyone interested in manufacturing, consulting, or operations careers.

Social Events

From catered meetings to volunteering, our social events bring students together for connection and community. Some examples include an outside BBQ, and a fun philanthropic collaboration with another Michigan organization -- Letters of Love. 

Department Wide Dinner

An fun annual tradition that brings together students, faculty, and staff all across IOE. Enjoy a free meal on us while engaging with our wonderful IOE community. In the past, the department wide dinner has been hosted at Pizza Cat, Cottage Inn, and more.
